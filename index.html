Published updates after video

  1. The platform now supports "stepwise" mode, enabling questions to appear sequentially, one after another.
  2. Question bank functionality has been implemented, allowing you to upload a larger database of questions (e.g., 100) and specify a smaller test count (e.g., 40). This ensures each test taker receives a unique, randomized selection from your question bank.
  3. Enhanced test isolation ensures test takers will only see quizzes from their assigned test creator, despite sharing a common URL. The system now dynamically generates unique experiences per test creator and their corresponding test takers.
  4. Unlike before, both th ename or email and the ID of your test taker are indicated in the Results for enhanced and easy identification of your test takers.
