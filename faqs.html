Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ExaminationHall

Is ExaminationHall really free to use?
Yes! ExaminationHall is free forever. You receive 3 examination credits per month with your free account. Additional credits can be purchased if needed.
What type of examinations can I create?
ExaminationHall specializes in timed multiple-choice questions. Each quiz must be completed within a specific timeframe that you set. We recommend watching our demo video on the homepage to understand the full functionality.
What is an examination credit?
An examination credit is consumed when you request the results of a completed examination to be sent to your inbox. Each free account receives 3 credits per month.
Are there any limitations on the number of test takers?
You can accommodate up to 300 test takers per month. There is no limit to the number of questions you can include in each examination.
Can I run multiple quizzes simultaneously?
No, you cannot run concurrent quizzes under the same account. You must complete one quiz and request results before creating another one. Any new quiz uploaded will override the previous one.
How do you handle feature requests and updates?
We welcome feature requests but carefully evaluate them to maintain the application's simplicity. We only implement updates that significantly enhance the core functionality without compromising ease of use.
What kind of support do you offer?
We provide support primarily through email. Our team strives to respond promptly during weekdays, though responses may be delayed during weekends. We prioritize addressing technical issues and user queries.
Can I purchase the application to run it independently?
Yes, you can purchase the complete codebase for $12,000. This allows you to host and manage the system independently on your own infrastructure.
How secure is the examination system?
ExaminationHall implements robust security measures to protect both examination content and user data. Each test taker receives a unique identification code, and results are securely stored and transmitted.
What happens if a test taker loses connection during an exam?
The system automatically saves responses as users progress through the examination. If a connection is lost, test takers can resume from their last saved point within the designated examination timeframe.
