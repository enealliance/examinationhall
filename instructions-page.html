Instructions for Test Creators & Test Takers

Essential guidelines for examiners to share with test takers to ensure a smooth examination experience.

For Test Creators

As a test creator, it's essential to provide your test takers with clear instructions to ensure they can access and complete their examinations without issues. Please share the following information with your test takers before the scheduled examination.

Additional Tips for Test Creators

  • Set clear time windows for your examinations that provide sufficient time for all test takers
  • Ensure your test takers have their unique IDs/passwords well before the examination
  • Consider running a practice test to familiarize users with the platform
  • Be available during the examination period to address any technical issues

Information to Share with Test Takers

Please read the following instructions carefully to ensure you can successfully complete your examination. The examination will only be available during the specified start and end times set by your examiner.

  1. Access: The test will be accessible via the test link provided by your examiner.
  2. Authentication: On the test page, you must enter your assigned password/ID exactly as provided, respecting capitalization and white spaces.
  3. Validation: After entering your password/ID, click "Validate". Upon successful validation, you will be able to select your assigned test from the dropdown menu.
  4. Time Management: The timer will start immediately when the test loads. You must complete all questions within the allocated time frame.
  5. IMPORTANT: Single Attempt Only - You have only one attempt with your ID. Do not refresh or reload the page during the test as this will prevent a second chance of validation or reloading the same test. The test must be completed once loaded.

Device Recommendations

The test is best taken on a laptop or desktop computer. Mobile phones may be suitable depending on the nature of the test. If the examiner has not included external links or downloads, a mobile device should work, but do not navigate away from the test page.

Examination Availability

The test will be automatically unlisted from the platform after the specified end time. Ensure you begin your test with enough time to complete it before the deadline.
